Photos

A "photo" is a shortened, informal term for a photograph, which is a picture created by capturing light with a camera, either film or digital. Essentially, a photo is a visual representation of a scene or object, usually created using a photographic process.

Background

The part of a scene or picture that is farthest from the viewer : the part of a scene that is behind a main figure or object in a painting, photograph, etc. Objects in the foreground are drawn larger than those in the background. a photograph of a house with mountains in the background.
